About Me

I am an Honours-level Data Science student at Eduvos, Johannesburg, working at NQF Level 8. I have a strong foundation in Python, data manipulation with Pandas, and machine learning concepts. I have completed several projects that demonstrate my ability to apply these skills to real-world datasets, including a credit card fraud detection model and a customer churn prediction system.

I am drawn to machine learning, analytics, fraud detection, and time series analysis. Each of these areas gives me a concrete way to work with messy, real-world data and produce results that matter. I study what happens before the model sees the data, because preprocessing decisions change everything downstream.

My goal is to work as a Data Scientist or Machine Learning Engineer in a team that takes data seriously. I want to build pipelines, ship models to production, and keep improving both my technical depth and my ability to communicate findings clearly to stakeholders.
